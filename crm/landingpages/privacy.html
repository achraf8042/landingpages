Privacy Policy

Your Data, Your Control - Complete Privacy with On-Premise Storage

Last Updated: December 6, 2025

Overview

DigiCare EMR is fundamentally different from cloud-based EMR systems. We prioritize your privacy and data ownership by providing an on-premise solution where all patient data remains exclusively on your local computer.

Key Privacy Principle

Your data never leaves your computer. DigiCare EMR does not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any patient health information (PHI) or personal data you enter into the system.

On-Premise Data Storage

DigiCare EMR operates entirely on your local computer without requiring internet connectivity or cloud storage:

  • Local Storage Only: All patient records, clinical notes, prescriptions, and appointments are stored in a local database on your device
  • No Cloud Transmission: No patient data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party services
  • No Remote Access: We cannot access, view, or retrieve any data you store in DigiCare EMR
  • Complete Control: You have full ownership and control over all data

Data You Control

When using DigiCare EMR, you are solely responsible for:

Patient Information

  • Patient demographics and contact information
  • Medical history and clinical notes
  • Prescriptions and medication records
  • Allergy information
  • Vital signs and measurements
  • Appointment schedules

Your Clinic Information

  • Clinic name and contact details
  • Provider credentials and signature
  • System preferences and settings

What We Collect (Software Usage Only)

DigiCare EMR Systems may collect limited, non-identifiable information for software improvement purposes only:

Optional Analytics (Can Be Disabled)

  • Software version and operating system type
  • Anonymous usage statistics (feature usage patterns)
  • Crash reports (no patient data included)

Important: All analytics are completely anonymous and do not contain any patient information or personal identifiable information. You can disable all analytics in Settings â†’ Privacy.

Data Security - Your Responsibility

Since DigiCare EMR stores all data locally on your device, you are responsible for:

Security Best Practices

  • Maintaining strong passwords for system access
  • Implementing regular data backups
  • Securing physical access to the computer running DigiCare EMR
  • Using encryption and security measures provided by your operating system
  • Ensuring compliance with applicable healthcare privacy regulations (HIPAA, GDPR, etc.)

Data Backup and Recovery

We strongly recommend:

  • Daily backups of your DigiCare EMR data directory
  • Storing backups on secure external media or encrypted cloud storage of your choice
  • Testing backup restoration procedures regularly
  • Maintaining multiple backup versions

Note: DigiCare EMR Systems does not provide backup services. You are solely responsible for data backup and recovery.

Compliance and Regulations

HIPAA Compliance (US Users)

As an on-premise solution, DigiCare EMR provides you with the tools to maintain HIPAA compliance. However, compliance responsibility lies with you as the data controller. You must:

  • Implement appropriate administrative safeguards
  • Use physical security measures for devices running DigiCare EMR
  • Employ technical safeguards (passwords, encryption, access controls)
  • Train staff on privacy and security protocols

GDPR (EU Users)

Since no data is transmitted to DigiCare EMR Systems, you serve as the sole data controller. Ensure you:

  • Obtain proper patient consent for data processing
  • Implement data minimization principles
  • Provide patients with access to their data upon request
  • Secure patient data with appropriate technical measures

Software Updates

When checking for software updates, DigiCare EMR may transmit:

  • Current software version number
  • Operating system type
  • License key for validation

No patient data or personal information is transmitted during update checks.

Third-Party Services

DigiCare EMR does not integrate with or transmit data to any third-party services. The software operates completely independently on your local system.

Data Portability and Deletion

Exporting Your Data

You can export data from DigiCare EMR at any time:

  • Clinical notes and prescriptions can be exported as PDF documents
  • The complete database file can be accessed directly in the data directory
  • No permission or access from DigiCare EMR Systems is required

Deleting Your Data

To permanently delete all data:

  • Uninstall DigiCare EMR application
  • Delete the data directory from your system
  • Securely erase backup copies

Contact Information for License

Your license key is linked to your purchase email address. This information is used solely for:

  • License validation
  • Providing software updates
  • Customer support communications

We do not share, sell, or distribute your contact information to third parties.

Children's Privacy

DigiCare EMR is designed for healthcare professionals. The software is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. Any patient data entered (including pediatric patients) is controlled solely by you.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via:

  • Email to your registered license email address
  • In-app notifications
  • Updates on our website

Continued use of DigiCare EMR after policy updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Your Rights

As the data controller for all patient information in DigiCare EMR, you have:

  • Complete ownership of all data
  • Right to access and export your data at any time
  • Right to delete all data completely
  • Freedom to move your data to other systems
  • No dependency on DigiCare EMR Systems for data access

Summary

DigiCare EMR is built on the principle of privacy by design. Your patient data remains exclusively on your computer under your complete control. We never have access to, collect, or transmit any patient health information. You are the sole controller and processor of all data entered into DigiCare EMR.
